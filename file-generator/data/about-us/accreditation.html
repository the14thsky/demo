Accreditation, Affiliation and Membership

SCBB is internationally accredited for its cord blood activities by AABB (formerly known as American Association of Blood Banks); its laboratory operations by College of American Pathologist (CAP) since 2010. We continue to be re-accredited biennially. For our collection of cord blood units for related and unrelated donations, accreditation from FACT-NetCord was achieved in 2014.

In line with its commitment to provide the highest standard in safety and quality, SCBB delivers its services through the practice of internationally accredited techniques of collection, processing, banking and disposition of cord blood units for transplants.

Affiliated to National Marrow Donor Program in the US, SCBBâ€™s public cord blood units are listed on Bone Marrow Donors Worldwide (BMDW), a global stem cell donor database. These units are accessible by Transplant Centres around the world, searching for matching cord blood units to facilitate a life-saving stem cell transplant for their patients.

SCBB is a member of World Marrow Donor Association since 2006. An association of international cell donor registries, WDMA provides a forum for members to discuss and share industrial expertise. SCBB is also a member bank of Net-Cord in recognition of our commitment to provide high quality cord blood units for transplantation through globally recognised standards and accreditations.
